How It Works

01

Connect Wallet

Connect your Web3 wallet (such as Slush Wallet or Demo Mode) to securely authorize your actions on the ReliefChain network.

02

Report a Crisis / Donate SUI

Submit disaster event details with location coordinates and evidence, or donate SUI tokens directly to active relief campaigns.

Evidence Analyze

All uploaded crisis media and budget credentials undergo strict analysis by the AI Evidence Authenticator to verify authenticity.

04

Fund Release

After governance admins review and approve campaigns or milestone delivery proofs, funds are securely unlocked to agents.

About the Mission

ReliefChain was built to restore trust in global disaster relief operations. Traditional aid organizations suffer from high administration overhead, delayed funding, and lack of transparency. By running entirely on decentralized infrastructure, we ensure that aid reaches victims in hours, not weeks, with every transaction visible to the public.
